{y′=f(x,y)y0=y(x0)\left\{\begin{array}{l} y^{\prime}=f(x, y) \\ y_0=y(x_0) \end{array} \right. {y′=f(x,y)y0​=y(x0​)​

欧拉法

用当前梯度作为函数的变化率进行迭代, 步长h=xn+1−xnh=x_{n+1}-x_nh=xn+1​−xn​, 精度不高

yn+1=yn+hf(xn,yn)y_{n+1}=y_n+hf(x_n, y_n) yn+1​=yn​+hf(xn​,yn​)

梯形法

用当前迭代点跟下一迭代点的平均值作为一次迭代的变化率

yn+1=yn+h2(f(xn,yn)+f(xn+1,yn+1))y_{n+1}=y_n+\frac h2(f(x_n, y_n)+f(x_{n+1}, y_{n+1})) yn+1​=yn​+2h​(f(xn​,yn​)+f(xn+1​,yn+1​))

改进欧拉法

在梯形法中, yn+1y_{n+1}yn+1​是未知的，无法直接求出来k, 因此可以先利用欧拉公式求出

yˉn+1=yn+hf(xn,yn)\bar{y}_{n+1}=y_n+hf(x_n, y_n) yˉ​n+1​=yn​+hf(xn​,yn​)

再带入梯形法中，得到yn+1=yn+h2(f(xn,yn)+f(xn+1,yˉn+1))y_{n+1}=y_n+\frac h2(f(x_n, y_n)+f(x_{n+1}, \bar{y}_{n+1}))yn+1​=yn​+2h​(f(xn​,yn​)+f(xn+1​,yˉ​n+1​))
